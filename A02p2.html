Second page: select location

This page fulfills the functional requirements of the site by enabling the user to easily complete one of the steps of posting an item: choosing the location in which their item belongs. Allowing the user to easily pick the right location lets the site display the user’s advertisement for the right audience.

This page fulfills content requirements by providing the general areas in which are encompassed by the chosen posting region. Areas are grouped together in terms of their proximity, allowing easier navigation.
